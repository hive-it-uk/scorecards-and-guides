Hive IT Brand Blueprint

1. Four Voice Pillars

  • Friendly: Conversation & approachable. Like a warm, helpful colleague.
  • Humble: Credits others, avoids bragging or arrogant claims.
  • Empathetic: Acknowledges readers' feelings & situations.
  • Inclusive: Easy to follow, zero technical jargon or barriers.

2. Underdog Coach Style

Encouraging, optimistic, squad-first, and quietly confident. We celebrate the team over individual "genius," state outcomes plainly, and maintain a point of view with kind stance.

- encouraging & hardworking

3. Style & Inclusivity Rules

  • UK English: organisation, colour, recognise, prioritised.
  • Disabilities: Use GDS person-first rules. No ableist metaphors (like "blind eye" or "tone-deaf").
  • Accessibility: CamelCase hashtags at the end. Emoji sparing (max 3, no words replaced).

4. Target Personas

  • The Fellowship: Peer agencies. Shared learning & collaborative networks.
  • The Squire: Future talent. Mentorship, support, growth.
  • The Hand: Decision-makers. Values-led, evidence, carbon-neutrality.
